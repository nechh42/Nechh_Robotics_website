Volatility Regimes Explained

May 5, 2026 • 5 min read • Strategy

Not all market conditions are equal. Our algorithms classify volatility into three regimes — each requiring different position sizing and stop placement.

🟢 Low Volatility (Compression)

Bollinger Bands narrow. ATR drops below 20-period average. Price chops in tight range.

Strategy: Reduce position size 50%. Wider stops. Wait for breakout.

🟡 Normal Volatility (Trend)

Healthy expansion. ATR within 1 standard deviation. Clean structure.

Strategy: Standard position size. Trend-following. Standard stops.

🔴 High Volatility (Expansion)

Bollinger Bands widen. ATR spikes 2x average. Whipsaws common.

Strategy: Reduce size 50% or flat. Tighter stops. Counter-trend forbidden.
