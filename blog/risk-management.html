The 5 Non-Negotiable Rules

May 6, 2026 • 4 min read • Education
01

Never Risk More Than 2% Per Trade

10 losing trades in a row = 20% drawdown. Recoverable. 10% per trade = destroyed account.

02

Always Use Stop Losses

Every signal we publish includes stop loss. No exceptions. "Mental stop" = no stop.

03

Let Winners Run, Cut Losers Fast

Average winner should be 2x average loser. Our algorithms target 1.5–3 R/R minimum.

04

Backtest Before Live Trading

Every strategy must survive 200+ trades of historical data before real capital.

05

Keep a Trading Journal

Review monthly. What worked? What didn't? Patterns emerge only with data.
