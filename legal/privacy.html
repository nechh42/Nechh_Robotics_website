Privacy Policy & KVKK Notice

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Nechh Robotics ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK No. 6698) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email address, password (hashed).
  • Payment Data: Transaction ID, subscription plan, billing date (processed by secure third-party providers).
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited.
  • Communication Data: Emails or messages you send to our support.

2. Purpose of Processing

We process your data to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Provide access to subscribed analysis services.
  • Process payments and send transaction confirmations.
  • Send important service updates (not marketing spam).
  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis (KVKK & GDPR)

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Explicit Consent: For specific purposes like marketing emails (you can withdraw anytime).
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our subscription agreement with you.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with Turkish accounting and tax laws.
  • Legitimate Interest: For service security and improvement (without overriding your rights).

4. Data Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only with:

  • Payment Processors: (e.g., Iyzico, Stripe) solely to complete transactions.
  • Cloud Service Providers: (e.g., Vercel, AWS) for hosting within secure jurisdictions.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by Turkish law or a valid court order.

5. Data Security

We implement technical (encryption, firewalls) and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights (KVKK/GDPR Rights)

You have the right to:

  • Learn whether your data is being processed.
  • Access and request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request Deletion (Erasure) of your data under certain conditions.
  • Restrict or Object to processing.
  • Data Portability.
  • Withdraw Consent at any time.
